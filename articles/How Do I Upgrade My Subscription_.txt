How Do I Upgrade My Subscription?
<h2>Upgrade Your Plan</h2>
<p>From the right dropdown menu, click on Account Settings. Next, click on Name &amp; Billing.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/4423870712589/mceclip0.png" alt="mceclip0.png"></p>
<p>Then on the Name&amp; Billing tab, select the pencil icon next to Current plan row:</p>
<p><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/4423870718221/mceclip1.png" alt="mceclip1.png"></p>
<p>Here you can now select the plan that is most suitable for you. Confirm your billing details to activate your new plan. That's it, congratulations!</p>
