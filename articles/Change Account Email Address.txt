Change Account Email Address
<h2>Change email address of your account </h2>
<div class="special-content special-content--important" style="width: 100%; margin: -10px auto; border: 2px solid #DB2960;">
<div class="special-content__icon"><img src="//p4.zdassets.com/hc/theme_assets/9802/200002237/support_icon-08.png" alt="Important"></div>
<div class="special-content__info">
<h3>Important</h3>
<p>Changing the email address of the Account Owner will not change the subscription details. In order to switch the ownership completely, please contact us to facilitate this process.</p>
</div>
</div>
<p> </p>
<p>Go to "My Profile" and then select the "Email Address" tab:</p>
<p><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/4424053007245/mceclip0.png" alt="mceclip0.png"> </p>
<p>Insert the new email address and click on "Verify":<br><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/4440994198797/mceclip0.png" alt="mceclip0.png"></p>
<p> </p>

<div class="special-content__info">
<h3>Tips</h3>
