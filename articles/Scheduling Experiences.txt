Scheduling Experiences
<h2>Introduction</h2>
<p>Convert has the feature to schedule your experiences to start on a future date, for example on a holiday.</p>
<h2>Scheduling your Experience</h2>
<p>After creating your experience and before changing the status from "Draft", you can define a date to start and activate the experience. This option can be found on  <strong><em>Experience Summary &gt; Status Button</em></strong> :<br><br><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/14272478855309"></p>
<p><br>Changing the status to Scheduled will trigger the calendar widget, just select the date desired for the experience to start:</p>
<p><br><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/14272481251085"></p>
<p><span class="wysiwyg-color-red"><strong>Note: The timezone used for scheduling is GMT.</strong></span></p>
