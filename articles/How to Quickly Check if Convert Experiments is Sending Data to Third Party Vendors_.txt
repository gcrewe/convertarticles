How to Quickly Check if Convert Experiments is Sending Data to Third Party Vendors?
<h2>Check Integrations Settings</h2>
<p>You might or might not want to send the data from Convert Experiments to Google, Crazy Egg, and other vendors.</p>
<p>To check this quickly, go into your Experiment Summary and more specifically to Integrations section.</p>
<p><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/14270648991757"></p>
<p>Select Add/Edit Integrations and see which integrations are enabled:</p>
<p><img src="https://support.convert.com/hc/article_attachments/4423885273101/mceclip1.png" alt="mceclip1.png"></p>
