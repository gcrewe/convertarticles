Adding Invoice CC's
<p>When you have multiple people that would like to receive the invoice, you can add their emails in the billing details of the Convert Experiences account.</p>
<p>In Account Settings &gt; Name &amp; Billing, we have added the option to modify all your billing details. You can add the company details, VAT and other details and the CC's for the invoice.</p>
