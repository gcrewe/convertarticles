Integrate Convert Experiences with Eulerian Technologies
<h2>Convert-Eulerian Integration</h2>
<p>Integration of <a href="https://www.eulerian.com/">Eulerian Technologies</a> with Convert Experiences will help you to import Convert data into your Eulerian Technologies Analytics account. </p>
<p>For each experiment, the integration passes along the experiment name and variation name that the visitor is currently bucketed into (if any).</p>

<h2>Add Tracking Code Snippets</h2>
<p>Make sure the Convert tracking code and the Eulerian Analytics code (eanalytics) are installed on your page. </p>
<h2>Activate Integration in Convert</h2>
<p>Go to your Convert experience settings and under Integrations select the Eulerian. Then click Save and Continue. </p>
