How Do I Stop or Start Variations During an Active Test?
<h2>Manage Variations During Testing</h2>
<p>You can stop variations during the experiment at any time.</p>
<p>To do this, you go to the Report and you stop a variation by clicking its status. This will pause the variation and will take this change into consideration on experiment results.</p>
